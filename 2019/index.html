100 Warm Tunas 2019

The internet's most accurate prediction of Triple J's Hottest 100 for 2019, updated daily.

We collect screenshots posted to Instagram and Twitter tagged with the hashtags #hottest100, #triplejhottest100, #triplej, and a handful of others to provide you with an accurate list of songs which could appear in the countdown. Submit your votes by uploading a screenshot and tagging it with one of these tags. You can also send it as a direct message to @100warmtunas on Instagram.

So far 16102 votes have been counted across 1784 images, equating to a sample size of about 0.65%+.

100 Warm Tunas is an experiment by Nick Whyte. Past results: 2018, 2017, 2016. 100 Warm Tunas was inspired by the work of the team who put together warmest100.com.au. Data was last processed Thursday 26 December, 2019 at 12:00 AM AEDT . Something not looking quite right? Check the latest news here.
