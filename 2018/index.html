100 Warm Tunas 2018

The internet's most accurate prediction of Triple J's Hottest 100 for 2018, updated daily.

We collect screenshots posted to Instagram tagged with the hashtags #hottest100,  #triplejhottest100,  #triplejhottest1002018,  #hottest1002018,  #triplej,  to provide you with an accurate list of songs which could appear in the countdown. Submit your votes by uploading a screenshot and tagging it with one of these tags. You can also send it as a direct message to @100warmtunas

Looks like you're here a little too soon! Watch this space, we'll be publishing the live leaderboard shortly after voting opens.

100 Warm Tunas is an experiment by Nick Whyte. Past results: 2017, 2016, 2015. 100 Warm Tunas was inspired by the work of the team who put together warmest100.com.au.
