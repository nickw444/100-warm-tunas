Can I vote for songs that aren’t listed on the voting website?

Of course! The triple j voting website allows you to enter details of songs that are not already listed. Just ensure they meet the eligibility criteria listed on the ABC website:

  • An eligible song must have had its initial release (online or on-air) between 1 December 2018 and 30 November 2019.
  • Covers, live recordings of previously unreleased songs and remixes are eligible.
  • If a song has been significantly reworked (edited/remixed/re-recorded) after its initial release, the reworked song is eligible. Live versions of songs by - and previously released by - an act are ineligible.
