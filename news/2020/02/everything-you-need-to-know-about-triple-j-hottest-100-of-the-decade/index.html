Everything you need to know about triple j’s Hottest 100 of the Decade

Feb 19, 2020 • Nick Whyte

Where can I vote in triple j’s Hottest 100 of the Decade?

You can cast your vote in the Hottest 100 by visiting the ABC website. Don’t forget to take a screenshot of your vote and send it to @100warmtunas on Instagram so we can count it in our predictions!

What date and time will the countdown be played?

The countdown will be aired on triple j on Saturday the 14th of March, 2020 and should start at around 12:00 PM AEDT.

When does voting close?

Voting closes at 12:00 PM AEDT on Monday the 9th of March, 2020.

Can I vote for songs that aren’t listed on the voting website?

Of course! The triple j voting website allows you to enter details of songs that are not already listed. Just ensure they meet the eligibility criteria listed on the ABC website:

  • An eligible song must have had its initial release (online or on-air) between 1 January 2010 and 31 December 2019.
  • Covers, live recordings of previously unreleased songs and remixes are eligible.
  • If a song has been significantly reworked (edited/remixed/re-recorded) after its initial release, the reworked song is eligible. Live versions of songs by - and previously released by - an act are ineligible.
